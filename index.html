Nebulas Types

Dark Nebula

Dense clouds blocking background light

Reflection Nebula

Reflect light from Nearby stars.

Emission Nebula

Glows due to ionized gas emitting light.

Planetry Nebula

Formed from outer layers of dying stars.

SuperNova Nebula

Expanding clouds of gas left after a steller explosion

Fun Facts about space

  • scientists predict the Andromeda galaxy will collide with the Milky way(our home galaxy),in about 8 billion years!
  • Time in space passes slower compared to Earth due to gravity and speed,meaning that the astronauts can return to Earth to find people there have aged more than them!
  • Astronauts can grow about 2-3 cm taller in space due to the lack of gravity,which causes the spine to expand slightly. this effect is temporary and reverses once they return to Earth!
